ABOUT THE DESTINATION

The Sacred Heart of Sri Lanka

Nestled in a bowl of verdant hills around the sacred Kandy Lake, Kandy is Sri Lanka's last royal capital and its spiritual soul. Home to the Temple of the Tooth Relic — Buddhism's most venerated site — this UNESCO World Heritage City is where ancient Kandyan culture, classical dance, and royal gardens breathe alongside a vibrant modern highland town.

Gateway to the legendary Kandy–Ella scenic train journey and the lush Knuckles Mountain Range, Kandy rewards visitors with layers of history, living tradition, and misty highland beauty that few destinations on earth can match.

Peradeniya Royal Botanic Gardens
Wander 147 acres of one of Asia's finest botanical gardens — a giant Java fig tree, an orchid house, a cannonball tree avenue, and sweeping views over the Mahaweli River.

Ambuluwawa Tower & Biodiversity Complex
Spiral up the unique multi-religious tower at Ambuluwawa, perched on a misty hilltop in Gampola — offering jaw-dropping 360° views over Kandy's highlands, cloud forests, and the valley below.

Kandy Lake & City Walk
Stroll the scenic lakeside promenade past the Cloud Wall, visit the Kandy Market, and soak up the colonial-era streetscape of Sri Lanka's hill capital at your own pace.

Udawatta Kele Forest Sanctuary
Wander through a royal forest reserve sitting directly above Kandy city — ancient meditation caves, towering fig trees, endemic birdlife, and a tranquil reservoir hidden inside a lush jungle canopy.
