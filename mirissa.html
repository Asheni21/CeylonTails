ABOUT THE DESTINATION

Where the Indian Ocean Meets Paradise

Curving gracefully along Sri Lanka's deep south coast, Mirissa is the island's most iconic beach destination — a perfect crescent of golden sand fringed by coconut palms and lapped by the warm, clear waters of the Indian Ocean. Relaxed, beautiful, and just the right size, it draws travellers who come for the sea and stay for the soul.

Beyond sunbathing and surf, Mirissa is the world's premier destination for blue whale watching, offers outstanding snorkelling and diving, and sits amid a string of charming coastal villages, sea turtle nesting beaches, and the historic fort town of Galle just 38 km away.
