ABOUT THE DESTINATION

Where the Wild Roams Free

Stretching across Sri Lanka's southeastern coast, Yala National Park is the island's most celebrated wildlife sanctuary. Home to the highest density of leopards in the world, Yala delivers raw, untamed encounters with nature that few destinations on earth can rival.

Beyond leopards, Yala shelters elephants, sloth bears, crocodiles, and over 200 bird species across a dramatic landscape of dry monsoon forests, lagoons, and ancient rock outcrops dotted with centuries-old ruins.
